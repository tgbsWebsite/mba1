THAKUR GLOBAL BUSINESS SCHOOL

Thakur Global Business School (TGBS) is a premier business education institute under Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET), a deemed-to-be university and is managed by Zagdusingh Charitable Trust. TGBS offers a 2-year MBA programme that develops future business leaders through experiential learning and real-world projects. TCET is recognised for quality education. It has Autonomous Status from the University Grants Commission (UGC) for 10 years from 2019–20, is accredited by NAAC, Bangalore, is ISO certified for quality, environment, and energy management, and offers programmes accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi.

    MBA Marketing

    Explore how brands are built, how customers make decisions, and how digital marketing works. This specialisation leads to careers in brand strategy, digital marketing, advertising, or starting your own marketing agency. You’ll learn how to create marketing plans that attract customers and build strong brands.

    MBA Human Resource Management

    Human Resource Management (HR) Learn how to hire the right people, help employees grow, and create a healthy workplace culture. This specialisation prepares you for careers in HR leadership, talent acquisition, employee development, or launching your own HR consulting firm. You’ll understand how to connect people strategies with business goals.

    MBA Finance

    Build strong skills in managing money, studying investments, and understanding how companies make financial decisions. This specialisation prepares you for roles in investment banking, financial consulting, corporate finance, or even starting your own finance-related business. You’ll learn how to analyse numbers, make smart financial choices, and guide a company’s financial growth.

    MBA Operations & Supply Chain

    Learn how products are made, moved, and delivered efficiently. This specialisation prepares you for roles in operations management, supply chain planning, logistics, or starting your own consulting business. You’ll gain the skills to improve processes and manage daily business operations smoothly.

    MBA Business Analytics

    Develop the ability to understand data and use it to solve business problems. This specialisation is ideal for careers in business analysis, data analytics, data science, or building your own data-focused business. You’ll learn how to turn information into useful insights that support better decisions.

    Eligibility Criteria

    Candidates must hold a 3- or 4-year undergraduate degree from a UGC-recognized university. Minimum aggregate marks of 50% (45% for reserved categories where applicable). Final year students awaiting results are eligible to apply subject to meeting the minimum criteria at the time of admission.

    1. UG degree (3/4 years) from a recognized university.
    2. Minimum aggregate 50% (relaxation as per category rules).
    3. English as a subject in Class 10 or 12 preferred.
    Read More

    Selection Criteria

    1. Minimum 50% aggregate in Graduation (3/4-year full-time degree in any stream from UGC-approved university; 55% for reserved Maharashtra category).
    2. Minimum 50% in Class 12 and Class 10 (English as a subject).
    3. Valid score in CAT, XAT, NMAT, GMAT, CMAT, MAH-CET, or MAT.
    4. Shortlisted candidates may be called for GD/PI as per programme rules.

    Admissions are merit-driven using standardized test scores; additional weightage may be given for academic performance, relevant work experience and performance in subsequent rounds where applicable.

    Duration

    The standard MBA programme duration is two academic years (four semesters) for full-time students. Certain executive or part-time variants have different durations; please check the specific programme page.

    • Full-time MBA: 2 years (4 semesters).
    • Executive/Part-time MBA: programme-specific durations.

    Scholarships

    Scholarships are merit- and need-based. CUCET scholarships cover a range of awards including partial tuition waivers and full scholarships for exceptional candidates. Scholarship decisions are made after assessing academic records and qualifying scores.

    • Merit-based scholarships up to 100% for top performers.
    • Special awards for candidate profiles matching industry needs.
